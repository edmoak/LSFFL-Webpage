Defending Champion Get Off My Ditka Prepares for the 2026 Season

The reigning champion returns to the fleet with every franchise aiming to take the title.

By The Navy Times Staff • 2026 Preseason

Get Off My Ditka enters the 2026 LSFFL season as the league’s defending champion after defeating the Saints in the 2025 championship game. The title defense begins with a new draft, a new schedule, and fifteen challengers preparing to make their own run at the trophy. As the league enters its twenty-seventh season, the standard remains the same: survive the draft, navigate the regular season, and earn the right to compete for an LSFFL championship.
