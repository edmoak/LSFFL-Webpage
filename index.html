Our Story

A League Built on Friendship and Tradition

In 2000, while stationed in La Maddalena, Italy, a group of sailors assigned to the USS Emory S. Land founded what would become the Lamad Squad Fantasy Football League.

What began with eight original franchises, handwritten draft sheets, and weekly league updates among friends has grown into a sixteen-team league spanning generations of family and lifelong friendships across the United States.

More than twenty-five years later, the competition remains fierce, the friendships remain strong, and the tradition continues.
