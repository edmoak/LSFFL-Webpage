Our Story

In 2000, while stationed in La Maddalena, Italy, a group of sailors assigned to the USS Emory S. Land founded what would become the Lamad Squad Fantasy Football League. What began with handwritten draft sheets and weekly league updates among friends has grown into a sixteen-team league spanning more than twenty-five years, generations of family, and members across the United States. Through every season, rivalries have flourished, champions have been crowned, and lifelong friendships have endured. Today, the competition remains fierce, the traditions remain strong, and the LSFFL continues to honor the brotherhood on which it was founded.
